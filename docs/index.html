About

This bots name is Flash Hunter, this bot will manage your schedule and work for you. It will find majors, work, and volunteers based on your interest. This bot will also manage your schedule by inputting date, time, and activities. You also can save the activities to your calendar. The commands include: !hello - say hi to the bot !commands - list of commands you can do !find-major - find a major based on your interest !find-work - find a job based on your interest !find-volunteer - find a volunteer based on your interest !time - add a activity to your schedule !ping/beep - for fun interaction
